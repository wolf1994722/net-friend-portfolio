Some important lessons I learnt in my college?

January 06, 2019 | 2 min read





    I am undergraduate student of National Institute of Technology, Warangal specialised in Computer Science and Engineering.

    These are some things, I learnt in my college life -

  • People will come and go in your life, nothing is permanent.
  • Keep your friendship circle small but real, Its always better to have 2-3 real friends who care for you in real than to have 10 people group where nobody gives a fuck about you.
  • Choose your seniors wisely, avoid wrong advices from wrong people otherwise your will lead to more troubles.
  • Dont prioritise your friends over family.
  • Dont depand on college - Not talking about tier 1 colleges, but in other colleges dont expect too much from them. They have selected you for only money.
  • People will use you and they will never help you back.(Exceptions can be there).
  • Never start drinking and smoking with your hostel friends, these are high chances that you will become addicted to it.



  • And always learn to say 'NO'.
  • Time management is important, start waking up without setting an alarm.
  • Think before you speak, a sharp tongue can cut your own throat.
  • Become more focused, your grades automatically will improve.
  • Use your library(not to study in the library) but at least issue books from there, you have paid for it.
  • Always remember, It is okay if someone breaks your heart, you can still survive.




    • Best of luck. :)
